Who We Are?

Solid Waste Management Board is a government organization that is responsible for the collection, transportation, and disposal of solid waste in the city. We are committed to providing efficient and environmentally friendly waste management services to our citizens. Our goal is to reduce the amount of waste that goes to landfills and promote recycling and composting. We work closely with local businesses and residents to ensure that waste is managed in a sustainable and responsible manner. Our team of dedicated professionals is always ready to help you with any waste management needs you may have. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you reduce your environmental impact.
